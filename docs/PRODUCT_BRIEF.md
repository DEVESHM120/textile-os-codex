# Product Brief

## Product

Textile Workflow Automation Platform

## Users

- Textile mill design teams
- Fabric technical teams
- Merchandisers
- Exporters and sourcing teams
- Fashion brand product developers

## Problem

Fabric development checks and production label documentation are slow, manual, and error-prone. Teams repeatedly exchange cloth cards, GSM, composition, construction, weave, and buyer-format information before sampling can move forward.

## MVP

One workflow that checks a fabric record and generates label output from the same data.

## Value

- Reduces feasibility review time from days or hours to minutes.
- Flags missing data and production risks before sampling.
- Produces consistent sticker and technical communication artifacts.
- Captures textile SOP knowledge in repeatable software.

## Demo Claim

From a cloth card to feasibility result and downloadable label workbook in under 2 minutes.
